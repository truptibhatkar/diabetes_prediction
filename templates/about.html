About Us

Our Vision

At diabetesprediction.com, we are committed to empowering individuals with precise diabetes risk predictions using advanced machine learning algorithms. By leveraging the Random Forest model, trained on a dataset of around 1 lakh individual records, we deliver highly accurate predictions with an accuracy rate exceeding 97%. Our mission is to foster proactive health management by offering users detailed insights and confidence percentages for every prediction.

What We Do

We specialize in utilizing machine learning to predict diabetes risk. Our platform analyzes health metrics such as blood glucose levels, BMI, age, and other factors to provide comprehensive predictions. These predictions are backed by extensive data from around 1 lakh individual records, ensuring accuracy and reliability. In addition to determining diabetes risk, we offer confidence percentages for each prediction, empowering users with actionable health insights. By blending data science and healthcare, we aim to support early diagnosis and prevention.
